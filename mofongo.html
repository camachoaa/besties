Mofongo

Mofongo is a very delicious dish full of flavor and texture, this dish can be made in different ways. example, mofongo stuffed with shrimp, pork chicharone,crab meat, beef, and vegetables. Mofongo is generally made from fried green plantains, which is mashed together with broth, garlic,olive oil, and pork cracklings or bits of bacon. It is often filled with vegetables, chicken, crab, shrimp, or beef and is often served with fried meat and chicken brothsoup.
